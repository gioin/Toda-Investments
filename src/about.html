About us

TODA Investments is a consulting company focused on innovative development of business, it provides a high-quality outsourcing consulting services in real estate investment and related fields based on the worldwide wealth of experience of the partners and its employees. TODA Investments helps local and foreign companies to invest productively and herewith assists the client to build the organizational structure in full compliance with acting international and local standards and legislation. TODA Investment also provides full legal service, including registration of corporate and non-commercial legal entities, researching legal issues, drafting, and analyzing various corporate and contractual legal documents, representation in the court.

Our Mission

TODA Investments is a reliable partner for business sector with current experience and futuristic vision of business management, making consulting a vital part of successful business. We work directly with the owners of companies and their senior staff, meeting regularly and getting to know your business from top to bottom to become part of your team.
